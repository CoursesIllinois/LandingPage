What?

CoursesIllinois lets teachers teach better and students work smarter with a streamlined announcement system, real-time feedback, and an efficient way to share materials. Your lives are stressful enough as it is. We save you both time and pain.

Teachers

  • Real-time feedback like never before

    Teacher's now have qualitative and quantitative feedback points based on instant feedback from students.

  • Ridiculously easy user interface

    Forget those stupid training sessions, this is so easy to use my grandmother can use it.

  • Integrates right into existing workflow

    Hate going to a website? You can do it straight from outlook or your inbox.

  • Pre-built course websites

Students

  • Streamlined announcement system

    Centralizes a system for announcements with notifications so you're always up to date.

  • Integrated with mobile devices

    Find the information you need to know wherever you are.

  • Study groups

    Easily connect with your classmates with Facebook integration -- in a non creepy sort of way.

  • Discussions

    A completely new approach to classroom discussions focused around question ranking and tagging so you can find the right questions when you need to.

Why?

The inspiration of our product came from the pain and frustrations of using existing management systems. Information about classes are fragmented, disorganized, and difficult to find. We want to build a brand new innovative system that is built to last.

Who?

CoursesIllinois is currently being built by talented students with the guidance and instruction of expert faculty members.
