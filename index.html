What?

CoursesIllinois lets teachers teach better and students work smarter. With a streamlined announcement system, real-time feedback, and an efficient way to share materials all centralized on one website, they can do just that. Your lives are stressful enough as it is.

CoursesIllinois is an alternative to Compass built by students and teachers.

Teachers

Access. Adapt. Answer.

  • Real-Time Feedback

    Instantaneous course evaluation and adaptation like never before. A new way to receive anonymous feedback from students.

  • Ridiculously easy user interface

    No training required, this is so easy to use my grandmother can use it.

  • Integrates right into existing workflow

    We know change can be annoying, so our plugins let you make announcements straight from Outlook or your inbox.

  • Pre-built course websites

    Consistency and easy set up allows for more time spent planning the curriculum than building a website.

Students

Connect. Control. Concentrate.

  • Streamlined announcement system

    A system so smart it makes your to-do lists and schedule for you. Versatile enough to send announcements through email, SMS, or our mobile application.

  • Integrated with mobile devices

    Students can remain connected, even away from the computer.

  • Study groups

    Easily connect with your classmates to set up study sessions to collaborate with each other. Works with Facebook integration so we can find your friends-- in a non creepy sort of way.

  • Discussions

    A completely new approach to classroom discussions focused around question ranking and tagging so you can find the right questions when you need to.

Why?

The inspiration of our product came from the pain and frustrations we face as students with existing management systems. Information about classes are fragmented, disorganized, and difficult to find. We asked other students if they had the same problems. Check out what they wrote in 24 hours below.

We want to build a brand new innovative system that solves these problems, and then some.
